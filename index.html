Meet Some Fascinating Species

Brilliantly colored and tough as armor, jewel beetles are masters of camouflage and survival.

With their gentle movements and surprising variety, tarantulas are more misunderstood than dangerous.

Fiddler crabs use their massive claw to communicate and attract mates along coastal shores.

Famous for their lightning-fast punches and incredible eyesight, mantis shrimps are true reef warriors.

In the small and the many lies the story of Earth's resilience. Without arthropods, life as we know it would collapse.

E.O. Wilson, Biologist
